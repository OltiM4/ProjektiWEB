Our Story

OMEK is an Albanian fashion brand inspired by vibrant youth culture, trends, street style and the urban world. The clothing brand was founded in 2015 and launched in 2017 and quickly expanded, currently offering worldwide shipment and physical retail stores on three different national markets. Furthermore, a well thought-out range of unisex as well as male and female clothing. All I had was a dream and ambition...
